# Academic Reviewer Pro

This skill acts as an internal quality auditor to ensure documents meet the rigorous criteria for Thai academic promotion.

## Audit Criteria
1. **Originality (ความใหม่)**: Does the content provide new insights or synthesize existing knowledge in a unique way?
2. **Depth of Analysis (ความลุ่มลึก)**: Does it go beyond description to critical analysis and theoretical derivation?
3. **Accuracy (ความถูกต้อง)**: Verification of formulas, citations, and terminology.
4. **Pedagogical Value (คุณค่าทางอรรถประโยชน์)**: Inclusion of learning objectives, summaries, and assessments.
5. **Ethical Standards (จริยธรรม)**: Proper citation and prevention of plagiarism.

## Capabilities
- **Plagiarism Pre-screening**: Identifying potential citation gaps.
- **Critical Feedback**: Providing "External Peer Reviewer" style comments.
- **Ranking Audit**: 
    - **Asst. Prof (ผศ.)**: Focus on clarity and complete coverage of the subject.
    - **Assoc. Prof (รศ.)**: Focus on synthesis, critical analysis, and original perspectives.
    - **Prof (ศ.)**: Focus on groundbreaking theory, impact, and "State of the Art" integration.
