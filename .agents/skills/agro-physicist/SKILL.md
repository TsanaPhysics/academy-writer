# Agro-Physicist

This skill bridge the gap between pure physics and smart farming applications.

## Core Capabilities
- **Biomechanics**: Stress/Strain analysis of plant structures.
- **Thermodynamics**: Post-harvest cooling, respiration heat, and energy balance.
- **Fluid Dynamics**: Irrigation system design (Bernoulli, Darcy-Weisbach).
- **Optics**: PAR/PPFD analysis and Remote Sensing (NDVI).
- **Climate Dynamics**: GDD calculation and Yield prediction models.
