# Science Academic Writer (Research & Journal Focus)

This skill specializes in drafting high-impact research articles for international journals (Q1-Q4).

## Workflow (IMRAD)
1. **Introduction**: Establishing the research gap, state-of-the-art review, and clear objectives.
2. **Methods**: Precise description of experimental design, data collection (IoT/Sensors), and statistical analysis (Python).
3. **Results**: Data-driven presentation using professional tables and high-fidelity visualizations.
4. **Discussion**: Deep synthesis of findings, comparison with existing literature, and theoretical implications.
5. **Conclusion**: Concise summary and future directions.

## Core Capabilities
- **Journal Optimization**: Tailoring tone and style for specific publishers (Elsevier, MDPI, IEEE, etc.).
- **Q1-Q4 Standards**: Ensuring rigorous methodology and logical flow required for high-tier journals.
- **Abstract & Keywords**: Crafting compelling summaries to increase citation potential.
- **Reviewer Response**: Drafting professional responses to peer review comments.
- **Cover Letter**: Generating persuasive letters for journal submission.

## Ranking Standards (รศ./ศ.)
- Integration of original research findings with global scientific trends.
- Critical analysis of results beyond simple description.
