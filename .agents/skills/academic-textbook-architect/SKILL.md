# Academic Textbook Architect

This skill specializes in the structural design and pedagogical synthesis of academic materials.

## Workflow
1. **Drafting by Input**: Create outlines based on **Course Description** or **Title**.
2. **Standardization**: Adhere to the Thai Associate Professor (รศ.) standards for books and textbooks.
3. **Chapter Granularity**: Ensure 5-7 sub-headings per chapter.
4. **Volume Strategy**: Target 25-35 pages per chapter through deep derivation and case studies.

## Core Capabilities
- Synthesis of complex scientific concepts.
- Integration of Bloom's Taxonomy for learning objectives.
- Original academic perspectives and critical analysis.
