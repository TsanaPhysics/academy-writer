# Agro-Physicist

This skill bridge the gap between pure physics and smart farming applications.

## Core Capabilities
- **Biomechanics**: Stress/Strain analysis of plant structures and mechanical stability.
- **Thermodynamics**: Post-harvest cooling, respiration heat, and energy balance.
- **Fluid Dynamics**: Irrigation system design (Bernoulli, Darcy-Weisbach) and Osmotic pressure models.
- **Optics**: PAR/PPFD analysis and Remote Sensing (NDVI/PRI) for vigor assessment.
- **Soil & Water Quality**: 
    - Physical and chemical parameter analysis (pH, EC, NPK, Dissolved Oxygen).
    - Nutrient transport physics and soil-water-plant relationships.
- **Pest & Disease Dynamics**: 
    - Physical modeling of pest populations and pathogen spread based on micro-climate data (Temp/Humidity).
    - Identification of structural damage caused by insects.
- **Plant Vigor Analysis**: Quantitative assessment of health using biophysical markers and turgor pressure.
- **Climate Dynamics**: GDD calculation and Yield prediction models.
